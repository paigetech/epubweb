SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION:

Proposal for the following collection of information:

OMB Number: 3133-0174.

Form Number: N/A.

Type of Review: Reinstatement, without change, of a previously approved collection.

Title: NCUA Economic Development Specialist Direct Assistance Survey.

Description: The survey will provide federally insured credit unions with an opportunity to give NCUA feedback on direct assistance provided by economic development specialists. NCUA will use the information to evaluate and improve the National Small Credit Union Program.

Respondents: Small Credit Unions.

Estimated Number of Respondents/Record keepers: 300.

Estimated Burden Hours per Response: 15 minutes.

Frequency of Response: Semi-annually.

Estimated Total Annual Burden Hours: 150 hours.

Estimated Total Annual Cost:$0.
