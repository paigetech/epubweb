This is a Notice of the Presidential declaration of a major disaster for Public Assistance Only for the State of ARIZONA (FEMA-1940-DR), dated 10/04/2010.

Incident: Severe Storms and Flooding.

Incident Period: 07/20/2010 through 08/07/2010.

Notice is hereby given that as a result of the President's major disaster declaration on 10/04/2010, Private Non-Profit organizations that provide essential services of governmental nature may file disaster loan applications at the address listed above or other locally announced locations.
