FTA has been collecting project evaluation information from project sponsors under the existing OMB approval for this program (OMB No. 2132-0561). However, due to modifications in the project evaluation criteria and FTA evaluation and rating procedures for the New Starts program and the addition of the Small Starts program, it became apparent that some information now required might be beyond the scope of ordinary planning activities. In particular, SAFETEA-LU creates additional requirements for before-and-after data collection as a condition of obtaining aFull Funding Grant Agreement (FFGA) or a Project Construction Grant Agreement (PCGA).
