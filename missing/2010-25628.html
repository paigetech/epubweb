SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION:

The information collected through these forms from small business loan applicants as well as participating lenders is used to determine eligibility for an ARC loan that is designed to assist small businesses with making timely payments on existing business data.

Title:“America's Recovery Capital (ARC) Loan Program.”

Description of Respondents: Participating Lenders to be eligible for an SBA guaranteed loan.

Form Numbers: 2315, 2316A, B, C.

Annual Responses: 11,600.

Annual Burden: 4,200.
